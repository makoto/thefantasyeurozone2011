★ The Fantasy Eurozone ★

What is this?

Greece is on the verge of getting dropped off the eurozone and some people think that's the end of the eurozone as a whole. What if Eurozone is like a football team, where players are constantly replaced to keep the team condition at best?

How to play?

Very simple. Either click links on the right side to see what countries consist of for each category. You can also click each country at the bottom to add/remove one and make your own fantasy eurozone.
